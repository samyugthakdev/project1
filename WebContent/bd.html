BLOOD DONATION
Overview:
Blood donation is all about gifting life to other people. In addition blood donation makes the work of blood banks easier.

BRING A LIFE BACK TO POWER

The blood you donate gives someone another chance of life.Give the gift of life.
Benefits:
Donating blood has benefits for your emotional and physical health.
It may also help you to reduce stress, help rid of negative feelings, reduce calories etc.
It gives you a sense of pride. It Preserve cardio-vascular health.It reduce the risk cancer.
Criteria for donation:
  1. The donor must be fit and healthy, and should not be suffering from transmittable diseases.
  2. The donor must be 18-65 years old and should weigh a minimum of 50 kg.
  3. Hemoglobin level should be minimum of 12.5 g/dL.
  4. Body temperature should be normal, with an oral temperature not exceeding 37.5 Â°C.
Ineligibility criteria:
  1. Have ever had a positive test for the AIDS virus.
  2. Had a tattoo in the past 3 months or received a blood transfusion in the past 3 months.
  3. Individuals who have had fits, tuberculosis,allergic disorders in the past.
